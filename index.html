Muhammad

Muhammad is the founding figure of Islam.

About Muhammad

  • Was born in Ur Kasdim, Kaldea, Sumer (Southern Iraq) on 2150 BCE.
  • Became aware that there was only one God (instead of many) at the age of 40.
  • Married his half sister Saral (Sarah).
  • Became a nomad, and left his hometown by God's orders, bouncing around from city to city, country to country
  • Was promised to by God himself that he would make Abraham a great nation
  • Confirmed by a convenant, was promised to by God that he and Sarah would have a son despite their old ages.
  • Had two sons, Ishmael by an egyptian slave when Abraham was 86 years old. And Isaac born by Sarah when Abraham was 100 years old.
  • As a test of faith, God demanded that Abraham sacrifice his promised son Isaac. Abraham complied.
  • At the very moment of sacrifice, God spoke to Abraham and spared Isaac.
  • After Sarah died, Abraham remarried to Keturah and had six more sons.
  • Abraham died at the age of 175.

The Covenant

Genesis 17:1-5 ~ When Abram was ninety-nine years old the Lord appeared to Abram and said to him, “I am God Almighty; walk before me, and be blameless, that I may make my covenant between me and you, and may multiply you greatly.” Then Abram fell on his face. And God said to him, “Behold, my covenant is with you, and you shall be the father of a multitude of nations. No longer shall your name be called Abram, but your name shall be Abraham, for I have made you the father of a multitude of nations.
